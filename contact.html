Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical response time for inquiries?
We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please contact us via WhatsApp for immediate assistance.
Do you offer virtual consultations?
Yes, we offer virtual consultations via Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or WhatsApp video calls to accommodate clients from different locations.
What documents do I need to provide for trade finance services?
Required documents vary by service but typically include company registration documents, financial statements, identification, and transaction details. Our team will provide a specific checklist based on your needs.
How long does it take to process banking instruments?
Processing times vary by instrument type and complexity. Simple instruments may take 3-5 business days, while more complex structured instruments can take 1-2 weeks.
