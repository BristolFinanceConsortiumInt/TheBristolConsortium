Service Details

Standby Letters of Credit

Our Standby Letters of Credit (SBLC) serve as financial guarantees that ensure payment will be made if a client fails to fulfill a contractual commitment. We provide SBLCs for various purposes including performance bonds, bid bonds, and advance payment guarantees.

  • Performance guarantees for contractual obligations
  • Bid bonds for tender participation
  • Advance payment guarantees
  • Financial guarantees for loan facilities
  • Customized terms to match your specific needs
  • Global acceptance and recognition
  • Swift issuance and processing
  • Competitive pricing structures

Bank Guarantees

Our bank guarantee services provide financial security for various business operations, ensuring that contractual obligations are met. We offer both direct and indirect guarantees tailored to your specific requirements across multiple industries and transaction types.

  • Tender guarantees for bidding processes
  • Performance guarantees for contract fulfillment
  • Payment guarantees for supplier assurance
  • Advance payment guarantees for project funding
  • Customized guarantee structures
  • International and domestic coverage
  • Risk assessment and mitigation
  • Expert advisory on guarantee requirements

Letters of Credit

We facilitate international trade through secure Letters of Credit (LC) that guarantee payment to exporters upon fulfillment of specified conditions. Our LC services include both sight and usance letters of credit, providing flexibility for various trade scenarios.

  • Sight Letters of Credit for immediate payment
  • Usance Letters of Credit for deferred payment
  • Revocable and Irrevocable LCs
  • Confirmed and Unconfirmed LCs
  • Transferable Letters of Credit
  • Back-to-Back Letters of Credit
  • Revolving Letters of Credit
  • Standby Letters of Credit

Our documentary credit services provide a secure payment mechanism in international trade, backed by comprehensive documentation handling. We ensure full compliance with UCP 600 and other international standards, minimizing risk for all parties involved.

  • Full compliance with UCP 600 standards
  • Comprehensive document verification
  • Risk mitigation through proper documentation
  • Expert advice on documentary requirements
  • Efficient processing and settlement
  • Global network of correspondent banks
  • Document preparation assistance
  • Compliance checking and validation
