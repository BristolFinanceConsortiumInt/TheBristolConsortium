Instrument Discounting

Our discounting services allow you to access immediate funds by selling your future payment instruments at a discount. This provides you with liquidity while the instrument holder assumes the risk and waits for the maturity date.

  • Discount Letters of Credit and Bills of Exchange
  • Immediate cash against future payments
  • Competitive discount rates
  • Flexible discounting terms
  • Risk assessment and mitigation
  • Document verification services
  • Quick processing and settlement
  • Recourse and non-recourse options
